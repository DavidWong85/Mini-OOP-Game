How to Play:
Use your mouse to control the cursor.
Use your Keyboard key 1,2,3,4 to switch cursor color.
1 for red, 2 for blue, 3 for brown, 4 for green.
Click the circle with your left mouse button or keyboard space.
If the color match, gain a point.
Else, lose a health.
You will have 3 health in total.
